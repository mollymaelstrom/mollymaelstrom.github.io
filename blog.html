Specialty Coffee Association of America

Seattle | April 2014

We'll be keeping this post short and sweet, but we thought that as bloggers and "tastemakers", you might want to know what our favorite bits of SCAA '14 Seattle Event were.

FAVORITE PRODUCTS

  • ModBar
  • Alpha Dominche
  • Slayer's Ninety+ Machine
  • Acaia Scale
  • Hario's Copper V60 Dripper Kettle and Pourover
  • Pacific Barista Series Almond Milk

FAVORITE COMPETITORS

  • Brewer’s Cup Caleb Lewis - Square One Coffee - 8th Place
  • Barista Competition - Nora Brady - Blueprint Coffee - 6th Place
  • Barista Competition - Laila Ghambari - Cherry Street Coffee House - 1st Place

FAVORITE BOOTHS ETC.

  • Slate’s Satellite Cafe
  • Espresso Parts and their Custom Shop
  • Collectivo Coffee
  • Slayer
  • Hario (they had ModBar’s pourover module)
  • ModBar Installation in Seattle Coffee Works

ADDITIONAL CLASSES

  • The Value of a Distinct Brand - Brian Jones of DCILY

And this concludes our SCAA coverage. We’re hoping to see many of your faces in Seattle this coming spring for the WORLD coffee competitions. Even if we failed so hard at media coverage they don’t want us back, we’ll be there one way or another. We wouldn’t miss it for anything!
